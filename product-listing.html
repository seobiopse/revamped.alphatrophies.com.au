Everything you need to know about buying trophies in Australia

Whether you're a sports club secretary ordering end-of-season trophies, a school coordinator planning an academic awards night, or a business looking for corporate recognition awards — this guide covers everything you need to make the right choice.

🏅
How to choose the right trophy for your sport
Resin figurine trophies are the most popular choice for sports clubs — they feature a sport-specific athlete figure on a base, making them instantly recognisable. Mini trophies (under 150mm) suit participation awards; full-size trophies (250mm+) are ideal for Best & Fairest and Player of the Year. For team sports like AFL, soccer, and cricket, most clubs order a mix: large trophies for 3–5 major awards, mid-size for individual milestones, and medals for full team participation.

Trophies for school awards nights and academic events
For school award nights, the most common combination is academic achievement trophies for top students, sport trophies for sports captains and award winners, and ribbons or rosettes for participation events like swimming carnivals and athletics days. Schools regularly order 50–200+ items per term — Alpha Trophies' Club Account is designed specifically for this, with saved templates, invoice billing, and priority production.

Corporate trophies and employee recognition awards
For corporate events and employee recognition programs, crystal and glass awards project a premium, professional image — they sit well on a desk and photograph well at presentations. Acrylic and timber plaques are popular for teams and departments. Alpha Trophies provides a free artwork proof for all corporate orders to ensure logos, names, and wording are exactly right before production begins.
